### 2. **Biochar Retort Kiln Design (100 kg/day Phase 1)**

**Feedstock sources:**
- Farm prunings: 60% (200 kg/week)
- Kitchen waste: 20% (15 kg/day)
- Laundry scrap fabric: 10% (6 kg/day)
- Wood from construction offcuts: 10%

**Kiln specs:**
- **Type:** Drum kiln (TLUD — top-lit updraft) or retort kiln
- **Capacity:** 100 kg/day (Phase 1), scalable to 300 kg/day (Phase 2)
- **Output:** 30–40 kg biochar/day (30–40% yield)
- **CAPEX:** ₹4–6L (simple steel drum, basic controls)
- **OPEX:** ₹2K/month (operator + maintenance)

**Revenue streams:**
```
Biochar sales: 30 kg/day × 25 days × ₹200/kg = ₹1.5L/month
└── Markets: Local farmers (soil amendment), export (carbon credits)

Carbon credits (VCS standard): ₹100–150/ton offset
└── 25 tons/year biochar × ₹125 = ₹3.1L/yr = ₹26K/month
```

**Total biochar revenue:** ₹1.5L + ₹26K = **₹1.76L/month by Month 12**

### 3. **Biogas Plant Expansion (Phase 0 → Phase 1)**

**Phase 0 (current):** 25 m³ plant (MNRE subsidised)
**Phase 1 (Month 7–8):** Upgrade to 50 m³ capacity

**Feedstock:**
- Kitchen waste: 50 kg/day → 1.5–2 m³ biogas
- Cow manure (if dairy added): 100 kg/day → 3–4 m³ biogas
- **Total potential:** 4–6 m³/day biogas

**Energy offset:**
- Kitchen needs: 3 m³/day (2 burners @ 6 hrs/day)
- **Monthly energy value offset:** ₹8–12K (₹400/m³ LPG equivalent)
- **Annual offset:** ₹1–1.5L

**CAPEX:** ₹8–10L (expand reactor, new piping, safety systems)  
**MNRE subsidy (40%):** ₹3–4L (automatic, for organic waste biogas)

**Digestate use:**
- Nutrient-rich compost: 100 kg/day dry (3 tons/month)
- Farm input cost replacement: ₹1.5K/ton = ₹4.5K/month
- Sales (packaged compost): ₹1L/month

### 4. **Organic Farm Phase 1 — Zero-Mile Supply Chain**

**Farm footprint:** 1.5 acres (designated in Phase 1 site plan)

**Crops (3-season rotation):**
1. **Summer (Mar–May):** Vegetables (tomato, chili, pepper, onion, leaf greens)
   - Yield: 200 kg/week
   - Kitchen use: 150 kg/week (40% of produce needs)
   - Surplus sales: 50 kg/week @ ₹30/kg = ₹1.5L/quarter

2. **Monsoon (Jun–Sep):** Rice, pulses, leafy greens
   - Yield: 300 kg/week
   - Kitchen + members: 200 kg/week
   - Surplus: 100 kg/week @ ₹25/kg = ₹2.5L/quarter

3. **Winter (Oct–Feb):** Root vegetables, brassicas, herbs
   - Yield: 250 kg/week
   - Kitchen + members: 150 kg/week
   - Surplus: 100 kg/week @ ₹25/kg = ₹2.5L/quarter

**Kitchen integration:**
- 150–200 kg/week zero-mile produce = ₹15–20K/month cost savings for kitchen

**Revenue model:**
- CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) subscriptions: ₹5K/month boxes (20 subscribers) = ₹1L/month
- Farmers market sales (Madurai, weekly): ₹15K/week = ₹60K/month
- Farm visits (agro-tourism): ₹200/person × 50/month = ₹10K/month

**Total farm revenue:** ₹1.7L/month by Month 12
