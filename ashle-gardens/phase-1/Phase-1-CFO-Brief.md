### 2. **Unit Economics (Monthly)**

Model revenue + OPEX for each business unit in steady state (Month 18):

#### **Laundry — 800 kg/day**
```
Revenue:
  - Hospital SLA @ ₹22/kg × 800 kg/day × 25 days = ₹4.4L/month
  - Hotels @ ₹25/kg × 200 kg/day × 25 days = ₹1.25L/month
  Total = ₹5.65L/month (conservative)

OPEX:
  - Salaries (20 FTE @ ₹15K) = ₹3L
  - Chemicals + utilities = ₹0.8L
  - Maintenance + parts = ₹0.3L
  Total OPEX = ₹4.1L/month

Gross Margin = ₹5.65L - ₹4.1L = ₹1.55L/month (27%)
Payback (₹80L CAPEX) = 52 months (5.2 years)
```

#### **Kitchen — 500 meals/day**
```
Revenue:
  - Hospital canteens @ ₹80/meal × 300 meals × 25 days = ₹6L/month
  - Catering @ ₹150/meal × 100 meals × 25 days = ₹3.75L/month
  - Campus dining (members) = ₹1L/month
  Total = ₹10.75L/month

OPEX:
  - Food cost (₹30/meal × 500 meals × 25 days) = ₹3.75L
  - Salaries (10 FTE @ ₹18K) = ₹1.8L
  - Utilities + rent allocation = ₹0.5L
  Total OPEX = ₹6.05L/month

Gross Margin = ₹10.75L - ₹6.05L = ₹4.7L/month (44%)
Payback (₹55L CAPEX) = 12 months
```

#### **Heritage Rooms — 8 units @ ₹1.2L/month each**
```
Revenue:
  - 8 rooms × ₹1.2L = ₹9.6L/month (gross rental)

OPEX:
  - Housekeeping (0.5 FTE per room = 4 staff @ ₹12K) = ₹0.5L
  - Utilities, linens, maintenance = ₹1.5L
  Total OPEX = ₹2L/month

Net Margin = ₹9.6L - ₹2L = ₹7.6L/month (79%)
Payback (₹85L–110L CAPEX) = 11–14.5 months
```

#### **Capsule Eco-Houses — 12 units (Phase 0+1)**
```
Revenue:
  - 12 houses × ₹2.4L/month (60% occ @ ₹5K/night) = ₹28.8L/month

OPEX:
  - Housekeeping (0.3 FTE per unit = 4 staff @ ₹12K) = ₹0.5L
  - Utilities, linen, maintenance = ₹2L
  Total OPEX = ₹2.5L/month

Net Margin = ₹28.8L - ₹2.5L = ₹26.3L/month (91%)
Payback (₹97–143L CAPEX) = 3.7–5.4 months
```

#### **Temple Attraction**
```
Revenue (from Deepa):
  - Café, tours, events, photos = ₹10L/month

OPEX:
  - Staff (6 FTE @ ₹12K) = ₹0.7L
  - Café supplies, maintenance = ₹1.5L
  Total OPEX = ₹2.2L/month

Net Margin = ₹10L - ₹2.2L = ₹7.8L/month (78%)
Payback (₹25–40L CAPEX) = 2.5–4 months
```
