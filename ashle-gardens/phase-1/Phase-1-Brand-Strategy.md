## Sprint Deliverables (Due June 27)

### 1. **NRI Investor Pitch Deck (30 slides)**

**Target:** Upper-middle-class NRI retirees (age 55–75, ₹5–20 Cr net worth, returning to India)

**Narrative arc:**
1. **The Opportunity** (4 slides)
   - Madurai demographics: 1.2M population, growing NRI returnee cohort
   - "Return to roots" trend: 35% of Indian diaspora considering return by 2030
   - Gap: No upscale, wellness-first retirement community in Madurai
   - Ashle Gardens: Country club meets revenue-generating campus

2. **Ashle Gardens Model** (6 slides)
   - 12 self-funding business units (not dependent on room sales)
   - Circular economy: every cost centre is a profit centre
   - Phase 1 by Month 18: 20 heritage suites @ ₹1.2L/month rental income
   - By Year 5: ₹4–6 Cr/month operating surplus

3. **Heritage Rooms — Investment** (4 slides)
   - Pricing: ₹1.2 Cr purchase OR ₹1.2L/month lifetime lease
   - 1,200 sqft, Athangudi tiles, teak, lime plaster, private courtyard
   - Included: meals, utilities, housekeeping, healthcare access
   - Exit: Resale value ₹1.5–2 Cr by Year 5 (30–40% appreciation)

4. **NRI-Specific Benefits** (3 slides)
   - FEMA-compliant structure (Meenakshi providing legal)
   - No inheritance hassle: rental income flows to heirs, property managed by Ashle
   - Return-to-India concierge: visa, banking, household setup, tax planning
   - Cultural anchor: Tamil heritage programming, temple proximity, classical music

5. **Financial Projections** (5 slides)
   - Phase 0 live (Month 6): ₹2.2 Cr/yr revenue
   - Phase 1 live (Month 18): ₹10–14 Cr/yr revenue
   - Operating margin: 40%+ (business units de-risk property sales)
   - IRR: 18–22% over 5 years (outright purchase vs. rental lease)

6. **The Ask** (2 slides)
   - Seeking ₹50–75L co-founder investment (Phase 0 completion + Phase 1 momentum)
   - Equity: 5–10% of company (negotiable per check size)
   - Use: Complete Phase 0, fund MSME loan bridge, pre-marketing for Phase 1
   - Timeline: Deploy by Month 6 (July 1)

7. **Competitive Differentiation** (3 slides)
   - vs. Pune retirement communities (too cold, not Tamil)
   - vs. Ayurveda resorts (no accommodation, seasonal guests)
   - vs. Hospital-attached clinics (no lifestyle, institutional feel)
   - Ashle Gardens: Lifestyle first, healthcare adjacent, self-sustaining revenue

8. **Risk Mitigation** (2 slides)
   - Land already secured (J-farm, no land cost)
   - Phase 0 profitability proven (laundry SLAs, kitchen catering in progress)
   - Conservative projections (revenue 60%, OPEX buffer 120%)
   - Personal guarantee from founder (₹X net worth)

### 7. **Competitive Analysis Update**

**Existing comparables:**
- **The Palms, Auroville** (eco-retirement, ₹1–2 Cr purchase): No business integration, high ongoing costs
- **Punarvasa, Bangalore** (senior living): Urban, high-density, no space for farm/circular economy
- **Arambol Beach Huts, Goa** (NRI returnee): Holiday only, no permanent residency option

**Ashle Gardens advantage:**
- Only heritage campus in Madurai with integrated businesses
- Revenue model de-risks property—no need to sell 100 rooms to break even
- Circular economy unique value prop (sustainability + economics aligned)
- Cultural authenticity (Tamil heritage, not generic "wellness")
