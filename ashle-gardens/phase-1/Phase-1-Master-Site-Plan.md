## Utilities & Infrastructure

### **Water Distribution Network**

```
        BOREWELL (north)
        │ (5,000 L/day)
        ↓
        TANK (100 kL, elevated)
        │
        ├─→ HERITAGE ROOMS (300 L/day)
        ├─→ CAPSULES (200 L/day)
        ├─→ KITCHEN (800 L/day)
        ├─→ LAUNDRY (5,000 L/day)
        ├─→ FARM (800 L/day irrigation)
        ├─→ TEMPLE POND (300 L/day fill)
        ├─→ TEMPLE AQUARIUM (200 L/day)
        └─→ COE / GENERAL USE (400 L/day)
        
        Total allocation: 8,000 L/day
        Capacity: 5,000 L/day (borewell) + 3,000 L/day (stored)
        
        GREYWATER TREATMENT
        Laundry output → Filter → UV → Irrigate farm
        (2,000 L/day reclaimed)
        
        RAINWATER (monsoon bonus)
        Campus roof + temple + farm → Cistern
        (100–200 L/day seasonal)
```

### **Electrical Distribution**

```
        MAIN GRID CONNECTION (NH-85, 0.5 km away)
        │ 300 kVA transformer
        ├─→ KITCHEN (40 kW peak — combi ovens, cold chain)
        ├─→ LAUNDRY (35 kW peak — 4 washers + 4 dryers)
        ├─→ WATER PUMP (5 kW, 6 hrs/day)
        ├─→ HERITAGE ROOMS (15 kW A/C, lighting)
        ├─→ CAPSULE HOUSES (10 kW A/C, lighting)
        ├─→ COE LAB (8 kW, machines + WiFi)
        ├─→ LIGHTING (path, temple, common areas) (5 kW)
        └─→ MISC (offices, security, equipment) (10 kW)
        
        Peak load: 128 kW (all simultaneous, unlikely)
        Typical load: 80–100 kW (realistic)
        
        BACKUP
        └─ Generator (50 kVA, diesel, emergency only)
            Fuel reserve: 500 L (2-day supply)
        
        RENEWABLE (future, Phase 2+)
        └─ Solar: 20 kWp on heritage room + kitchen roofs
            (50% energy offset target)
```

### **Solid Waste Management**

```
        ORGANIC WASTE (kitchen + farm)
        │ 80 kg/day
        ├─→ Biogas plant (50 kg/day)
        └─→ Compost piles (30 kg/day)
        
        WET WASTE (laundry, greywater)
        │ Lint (15 kg/day), biosolids (10 kg/day)
        └─→ Compost piles + biochar feedstock
        
        DRY WASTE (packaging, glass, plastic)
        │ 20 kg/day
        ├─→ Recycling (glass to recycler, ₹5/kg)
        ├─→ Plastic ban (supplier contracts)
        └─→ Waste segregation bins (color-coded)
        
        SEWAGE (human)
        │ 100–150 person-days
        └─→ Septic tank → Soak pits (dispersal)
            Testing: Quarterly water quality
```
