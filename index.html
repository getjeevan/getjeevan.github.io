Ashle Gardens

📍 Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India

A heritage integrated campus with 12 business units — country-club retirement living, commercial laundry, central kitchen, eco-tourism, biochar, biogas, organic farm, COE innovation lab, co-working & research residency, pet spa & hostel, Ayurveda spa, and NRI concierge. Located at T. Kadambankulam, Kariapatti Taluk, Virudhunagar District — 22.4 km south of Madurai, 12.7 km from IXM Airport.

Project Pillars

12 business units — every cost centre is a profit centre

🏡

Retirement Community

Heritage suites + capsule Airbnb eco-houses + 3-acre lake + 1-acre island. Country-club living rooted in Madurai's heritage.

🧺

Commercial Laundry

B2B to 50+ Madurai hospitals, 200+ hotels, Sivakasi industry. RFID-tracked, NABH-compliant. ₹2.1 Cr/yr at scale.

🍽️

Commercial Kitchen

FSSAI central kitchen — hospital canteens, heritage catering, cloud kitchen. Zero-mile farm produce. ₹3–4 Cr/yr.

🌿

Eco-tourism

Day visits, MICE corporate offsite, school science programs, NRI visit packages. Farm + lake + biochar experiences.

♻️

Biochar + Biogas

Farm waste → biochar (soil + carbon credits) + biogas (kitchen fuel). Campus runs carbon-negative by Year 3.

🌾

Organic Farm

15 acres — Seeraga Samba paddy, heritage orchard, kitchen garden. Zero-mile food for all 100 residents.

🎓

COE Innovation Lab

Pro bono residency for brilliant young minds — solve real campus problems, free stay + meals + ₹50K prototype budget.

💻

Co-working & Research

Stay-and-work campus for entrepreneurs and scholars. ₹45K/month — desk + capsule eco-house + all meals.

🐾

Pet Spa & Hostel

Premium grooming including Ayurveda pet treatments + boarding. 74% EBITDA margin — fastest payback (9 months).

🧘

Ayurveda Spa

Heritage wellness — retreat programs, day spa, naturopathy. Extends to Ayurveda pet treatments (unique in TN).

🌐

NRI Concierge

Return-to-India advisory — banking, FEMA, property management, household setup. Retainer model for Tamil diaspora.

📍

The Land

T. Kadambankulam, Kariapatti Taluk, Virudhunagar Dist. 4.595 acres documented (expanding to 30–40 acres). 22.4 km from Madurai.
